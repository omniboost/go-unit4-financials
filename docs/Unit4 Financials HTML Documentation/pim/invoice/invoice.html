BalanceType 0 1 typePimInvoiceBalanceType

The balanceType of the invoice is dependent upon the balanceStatus and follows a simple mapping algorithm. By default when an invoice has been posted from Input, its balanceType is 'Not Attempted'. An invoice with a balanceStatus of Unbalanced has balanceType 'Not Balanced'. An invoice with a balanceStatus of Part Balanced has balanceType 'Not Balanced'. An invoice with a balanceStatus of FullyBalanced has a balanceType of either 'Adjusted', 'AdjustedByRFC' or 'Exact' dependent upon how the balance was achieved - was it exact or did it need to be adjusted with the use of discrepancies?
